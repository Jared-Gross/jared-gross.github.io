PD in Portage la Prairie: Understanding the True History between Indigenous and Hutterite Peoples


This professional development session focused on student-centered learning approaches, emphasizing the use of portfolios and inquiry-based learning to strike a delicate balance. It also explored the importance of student self-reflections, whether oral or written, in the assessment process. Additionally, the session delved into understanding perspectives on Hutterite-Indigenous relations, particularly in the context of river colonies. The discussion covered the history of Hutterites in Canada, the significance of Treaty education for Hutterites, and the importance of presenting a balanced narrative that acknowledges both emotional and factual aspects of the true history.
